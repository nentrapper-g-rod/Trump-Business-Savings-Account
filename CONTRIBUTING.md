# Contributing to the Trump Business Savings Account Proposal

Thanks for your interest in improving this policy proposal.

## Ways to Contribute

### 1. Economic Analysis
- Spot an error in the math? Open an issue.
- Have better data on small business cash flow patterns? Share it.
- Know the actual Treasury borrowing costs? Let us know.

### 2. Policy Refinement
- See a regulatory hurdle we missed? Flag it.
- Know how similar programs have worked (or failed)? Tell us.
- Have experience with FDIC, Treasury, or IRS implementation? We need your input.

### 3. Outreach
- Know someone in Congress, Treasury, NEC, or a think tank? Make an intro.
- Have media contacts? Help spread the word.
- Run a small business organization? Let's talk.

### 4. Documentation
- Improve the writing
- Create visualizations
- Translate to other formats

## How to Contribute

1. **Open an Issue** — For questions, suggestions, or problems
2. **Submit a Pull Request** — For direct improvements to documents
3. **Email Josh** — josh@ehrecycling.com — For partnership or outreach opportunities

## Ground Rules

- Keep it constructive
- Focus on making the policy better
- No partisan attacks — this is about helping small businesses
- Back up claims with data when possible

## What We're NOT Looking For

- General political commentary
- Unrelated policy proposals
- Spam or self-promotion
