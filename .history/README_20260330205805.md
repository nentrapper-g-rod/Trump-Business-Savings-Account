# Trump Business Savings Account (TBSA)
### A Market-Based Tax Timing Solution for American Small Businesses

**Version 1.0** | March 2026

---

## The Problem

Every quarter, 30+ million small business owners scramble to carve out cash to pay the IRS. It's not that we don't want to pay our taxes — it's that the timing doesn't match how money actually flows through a small business.

Cash comes in waves. Tax bills come on a fixed schedule. The mismatch creates stress, forces bad decisions, and holds back growth.

## The Solution

**Let businesses save for taxes the way we save for retirement.**

The Trump Business Savings Account (TBSA) allows businesses to:
- Deposit funds throughout the year when cash flow allows
- Earn interest on those deposits
- Pay quarterly taxes directly from the account
- No more panic. No more scramble.

## How It Works

### For Small Businesses
- Open a TBSA at any participating private bank
- Deposit when you have cash — weekly, monthly, whatever works
- Earn competitive interest (T-bill rate + premium)
- IRS withdraws your quarterly payment automatically
- Keep the interest you earned

### For Large Corporations
- Same structure, but at scale
- Tax-timing flexibility + interest on money you'll owe anyway
- Modest benefits, but enough to bring them to the table
- **Their deposits create the scale that funds premium rates for small businesses**

### For Treasury
- Deposits flow in throughout the year (not just quarterly spikes)
- Treasury borrows less short-term debt
- Reduced borrowing = reduced interest expense
- **Net savings: $0.5–2B annually**
- The program funds itself

## Key Features

| Feature | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **No Tax Increases** | This is timing, not rates |
| **No Mandates** | 100% voluntary participation |
| **No IRS Bureaucracy** | Private banks run the accounts |
| **Revenue Neutral** | Treasury saves on avoided borrowing costs |
| **Pilot-Ready** | Test it small, scale if it works |

### Rate Structure (Illustrative)

| Account Type | Base Rate | Lockup Bonus | Effective Rate |
|--------------|-----------|--------------|----------------|
| Small Business | T-bill + 50 bps | — | ~4.2% |
| Corporate (no lockup) | T-bill + 10 bps | — | ~3.8% |
| Corporate (3-year) | T-bill + 10 bps | +10 bps | ~3.9% |
| Corporate (5-year) | T-bill + 10 bps | +25 bps | ~4.0% |

*Rates illustrative based on current T-bill yields. Actual rates set by Treasury.*

## Why This Matters

> "When small business owners feel safe, we get more innovative. We take risks. We hire. Knowing my tax burden is taken care of would make me want to be more productive, not less. Multiply that by 30 million small businesses and you've got real economic growth — not from Washington picking winners, but from Main Street feeling secure enough to go build something."

## Implementation

### Phase 1: Pilot Program
- Select 3–5 states with strong small business ecosystems
- Partner with 5–10 regional banks
- Cap at $5B total deposits
- 18-month test period

### Phase 2: Evaluation
- Measure participation rates
- Track Treasury savings
- Survey business owner satisfaction
- Assess bank operational costs

### Phase 3: National Rollout
- Expand to all 50 states
- Open to all FDIC-insured banks
- Scale deposit caps based on demand
- Full integration with IRS systems

## What's NOT in This Proposal

- ❌ No tax rate changes
- ❌ No new IRS bureaucracy
- ❌ No mandatory participation
- ❌ No government-run accounts
- ❌ No increase in federal spending
